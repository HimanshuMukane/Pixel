About Us

In this era of digital revolution where everything is at fingertips, people are racing to learn, access and master these technologies as much as possible thus it becomes a great opportunity for we students to develop our skills and commands on it. Taking advantage of it we, the students of SAKEC are conducting the grand TECH CONCLAVE “PIXEL 2022” to provide the growing future techies of SAKEC a clear comprehension of the Digital World. The icing on the cake is, to enhance your experience eminent and noteworthy speakers of technical and entrepreneurship field are going to influence you and contribute their knowledge to take your skills to the next level. They will put forth their experience and make you fearless for your upcoming journey in this field. This event will focus on the technical aptitude development of students along with guiding them to make their right approach while making any choices in future technical fields. PIXEL is ambitious to invite and accept any form of ideas context postulates and implement them in the most rightful way leading to betterment of future TECH ENTHUSIASTS.
